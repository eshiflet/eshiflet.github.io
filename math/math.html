Math Drills For Kids



I taught after school math sessions for my daughter's class and created these worksheets for the kids. The kids actually had a lot of fun doing them, especially when the class raced against the clock and each other, and kept asking for more. I figure other kids and parents might want them too, so here they are. These are pdf files that you can print out. Of course, there are no copyright restrictions so feel free to print as much as you want and distribute.
